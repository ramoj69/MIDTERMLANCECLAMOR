About Us

Welcome to COFFYHAN, where every cup tells a story and every sip ignites a moment of bliss. Nestled in the heart of DRUGMARINAS, our passion for exceptional coffee drives everything we do.

Founded by a group of coffee Students with a shared dream of creating a haven for coffee enjoyers, COFFYHAN is more than just a café; it’s a community. Our journey began with a simple belief: that the perfect cup of coffee has the power to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences.

At COFFYHAN, we take pride in sourcing the finest beans from around the world, carefully selecting each variety for its unique flavor profile and quality. Our skilled baristas then expertly roast and brew these beans to perfection, ensuring that every cup delivers unparalleled freshness and flavor.

But COFFYHAN is more than just great coffee; it’s a place where connections are made, ideas are born, and memories are cherished. Whether you’re catching up with friends, fueling your creativity, or simply seeking a moment of tranquility in a bustling world, our warm and inviting atmosphere provides the perfect backdrop for your coffee journey.

So come, join us at COFFYHAN, where every cup is a celebration of life, love, and the pursuit of great coffee. Let’s create something beautiful together, one sip at a time.
